SAMOSA RECIPE

This is a large and tasty samosa

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Salt
  • Red meat
  • Wheat flour
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Grind the red meat and turn it into small pieces
  2. Take a half liter of luke warm Water, add 1 table spoon of salt and 1 kg of wheat flour, mix and kneed until the dough is ready
  3. Take a half liter of luke warm Water
  4. Roll the dough with a rolling stick and cut several pieces of dough papers
  5. inside each dough paper add the grinded pieces of meat and cover with wheat flour paste
  6. fry in the hot oil and watch the samosas changing the color
  7. Wait for samosas to cool and enjoy their taste
