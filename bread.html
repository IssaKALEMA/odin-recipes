BREAD RECIPE

This is a soft delicious bread that can be taken with any kind of tea or juice.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Yeast
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Take a half liter of luke warm Water
  2. Add 1 table spoon of yeast to the luke warm Water and mix
  3. Add 2 table spoons of sugar, a pinch of sugar and mix
  4. Add 3 quaters kg of Wheat flour and mix
  5. Add some extra flour, luke warm Water and oil if necessary in order to make the dough a bit soft
  6. Cover the dough and wait for 45 minutes
  7. Bake inside the oven for 45 minutes
  8. Enjoy the taste of your bread
